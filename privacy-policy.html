🛡️ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16/04/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Genel Muhasebe Asistanı, developed by Lastrigon App ("Application", "App", "we", "us"), may process information when you use the mobile application. The Application has recently introduced leaderboard, mini tests, profile-based progress tracking, reminder notifications, analytics, ads, and in-app update features. For this reason, this policy has been updated to describe these functions clearly and transparently.

Core educational features of the Application can be used without creating a classic account. However, some optional features such as leaderboard and cloud-based score tracking may require Google Sign-In.

1. Information We May Process

1.1. Information Stored Locally on the Device

The Application may store certain usage and progress data locally on your device, such as:

  • lesson/module progress,
  • selected mini test profile,
  • mini test completion states,
  • best correct answer counts,
  • profile-based solve counts,
  • last app open date,
  • notification pause state and reminder-related preferences.

This local data is used to provide progress indicators, remember your preferences, and personalize the in-app learning experience.

1.2. Information Processed for Leaderboard and Sign-In

If you choose to use the leaderboard feature, the Application may use Google Sign-In and Firebase services. In this case, the following data may be processed and stored for leaderboard functionality:

  • Google account authentication result,
  • your chosen display name / nickname,
  • your e-mail address,
  • your score-related data (for example leaderboard and point totals).

This processing is limited to enabling sign-in, creating or updating your leaderboard record, and showing score-based ranking features.

1.3. Analytics and Performance Information

The Application uses Firebase Analytics. Certain app events may be logged to understand app usage and technical performance. This may include events related to update prompts, update acceptance/cancellation, and update installation flow.

1.4. Advertising

The Application uses Google Mobile Ads / AdMob to display advertisements. Google may process certain technical data under its own privacy terms to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

1.5. Reminder Notifications

If notification permission is granted, the Application may schedule daily study reminders. Reminder content may be personalized based on app activity, such as whether you solved mini tests, which profile you used most, whether a profile has been completed, or how long you have been inactive.

1.6. In-App Updates

The Application may use Google Play's in-app update system. After the app has been open for a certain period, an in-app update prompt may be shown. If an update is downloaded, you may later be prompted to install it.

2. What We Do Not Collect by Default

For normal educational use of the core app, we do not require:

  • payment card information,
  • bank account information,
  • home address,
  • phone number,
  • traditional registration with a standalone username/password system.

However, if you choose to use optional features such as leaderboard, some sign-in related data (such as e-mail address and chosen display name) may be processed through Google/Firebase services.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play In-App Updates
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

These services may process data under their own privacy terms. We do not control the full internal processing performed by these providers.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Notifications and Inactivity Logic

The Application may avoid sending the same reminder type multiple times on the same day. In cases of extended inactivity, reminders may be paused and later resumed when the user returns to the app.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to support secure use of the Application and rely on established third-party platforms such as Google Play and Firebase for some technical functions. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure at all times.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version becomes effective when it is published.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

  • lastrigon@gmail.com
